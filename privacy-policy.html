Privacy Policy StepTop

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Richard ("we", "us") developed the StepTop app. This page informs you about our policy regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

2. What data do we collect?

StepTop collects the following data, stored exclusively on your device:

  • Step data: StepTop reads steps (read-only) from Google Health Connect on your device.
  • Settings: Your personal preferences such as daily goal, language, and notification preferences.
  • Notes: Personal notes you add per day, week, month, or year.

3. How do we use your data?

All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers or shared with third parties, except as described under advertisements (see point 5).

4. Health Connect

StepTop only requests read access to step data via Google Health Connect. The app never writes data to Health Connect. Data is not stored outside your device or shared.

5. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

StepTop uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. This is outside our control. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. No account required

StepTop does not require an account, registration, or login. No personal identification data such as name, email address, or phone number is collected.

7. Deleting your data

You can delete all StepTop data by uninstalling the app from your device. This permanently deletes all locally stored settings and notes.

8. Notifications

StepTop can optionally send a daily reminder notification. You can enable or disable this in the app settings. No data is transmitted when a notification is sent.

9. Required permissions (Health Connect)

StepTop requests the following permissions:

  • Read steps: to display your daily steps and calculate statistics.

The app never requests write permissions for health data.

10. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We advise you to review this policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective once published on this page.
